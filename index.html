Everything You Need

Publishing-quality output,
without the complexity

Every feature is designed for writers, not designers. Set it once, export beautifully every time.

✍️
Write in Plain Markup

Use simple symbols — # for chapters, ** for bold, __ for underline. No HTML, no complicated editors. Just write.

🎨
Full Typography Control

11 professional fonts including Garamond, Lora, Playfair Display, and Merriweather. Independent font and colour for every heading level.

📑
Automatic Table of Contents

Every heading becomes a clickable TOC entry with dotted leaders and page numbers — generated automatically on export.

📐
Precision Margins

Set margins in mm, inches, centimetres, or points. Six page sizes including Letter, A4, A5, and 6×9" trade paperback.

🔢
Smart Page Numbering

Roman numerals (i, ii, iii) for front matter, arabic numbers starting at 1 from Chapter 1. Just like professionally published books.

🖼️
Cover Images

Upload front and back cover images for a complete, print-ready PDF. Full bleed, no white borders, exactly like a published book.

Simple Process

From words to published PDF
in four steps

1
Write Your Content

Type or paste your manuscript using simple markup. Chapters, headings, bold, italic — all with keyboard symbols.

2
Choose Your Style

Pick fonts, colours, page size, and margins from the sidebar. The live preview updates instantly as you design.

3
Preview Instantly

Click Preview PDF to see your exact output in a new tab before downloading. Tweak until it's perfect.

4
Download & Publish

Hit Download PDF and get a professional, print-ready file named after your book. Ready to upload anywhere.
